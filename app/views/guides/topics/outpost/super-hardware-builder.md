# Becoming a Super Hardware Builder

To qualify for [Outpost](https://outpost.hackclub.com), you'll need to get Super Hardware Builder status!

You can get Super Hardware Builder status by submitting any hardware projects you've built! They can be from past programs, personal projects, or new projects you've made! The only bar is whether or not you made it yourself as your own design.

Deadline of July 7 to submit!

**Submit your projects [here!](https://forms.hackclub.com/submit-showcase-project)**

_any questions? ask in [#outpost](https://hack.af/outpost-slack)!_
